Feature Extractor: Regression - Distance Control

Control the size of a circle by moving closer to or farther from the camera.

  1. Lean back from the camera, set the slider to 0, and click "Add Sample" several times.
  2. Lean in close to the camera, set the slider to 1, and click "Add Sample" several times.
  3. Optionally, add samples at middle distances with the slider around 0.5.
  4. Click "Train" and wait for training to complete.
  5. Move closer or farther from the camera — the yellow circle will grow and shrink in real time!
